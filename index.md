Hello!

During my undergrad, I was advised by [Dr. Cynthia Rudin](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCFAkntpj2aOBJ1q4o4FhVwQ/featured) in her [Interpretable ML Lab](https://users.cs.duke.edu/~cynthia/).
